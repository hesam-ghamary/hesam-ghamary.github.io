Who I am

I'm a software engineer with nearly 6 years of experience across a wide range of domains from game development and mobile to frontend and backend engineering. That breadth of exposure gave me a strong foundation and a clear sense of where I work best. Over the past 2 years, I've narrowed my focus to designing ultra-low latency HFT services, where performance isn't just a feature, it's the product. This is the space I'm most passionate about and where I want to continue growing. More recently, I've developed a growing interest in systems development and low level programming, drawn by the challenge of understanding and optimizing what happens closer to the metal. I'm always looking to connect with engineers and teams pushing the boundaries of performance critical systems.

When I'm not writing code, I'm reading about computer architecture, system desing patterns, or exploring new programming paradigms.
